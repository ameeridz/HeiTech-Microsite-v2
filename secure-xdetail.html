Secure-X is a cybersecurity services provider offering a comprehensive approach to security.

Pain Points:

  • Lack of Cybersecurity Incident Response Plan: 77% of organizations do not have a Cybersecurity Incident Response Plan in place.
  • Slow Detection of Data Breaches: It takes companies an average of 6 months to detect a data breach.
  • High Cost of Data Breaches: The average cost of a data breach among companies globally in 2021 was $4.24 million.

The Solution - Secure-X's R.A.V.E Approach

  • Be (R)eady: Secure-X helps organizations be ready for upcoming cyber threats.
  • Be (A)gile: Secure-X has an adaptive security architecture to predict, prevent, detect, and correct potential cybersecurity issues.
  • Be (V)igilant: Secure-X helps organizations assess, measure, and formulate strategies to reduce cyber exposure.
  • Be (E)fficient: Secure-X's IT SecOps supports continuous monitoring, hunting, response, remediation, and recovery of lost data.

Secure-X Services:

  • curePod: NexGen SOC with Risk-Based Vulnerability Management (RBVM) to increase protection from cybersecurity threats.
  • practicePod: Manage policies, controls, risks, assessments, and deficiencies across the entire business.
  • prescribePod: Tailor the organization's cybersecurity design landscape according to best practices and proven technology.
  • cyberKnowledgePod Bridge the organization's cybersecurity skills gap with exclusive training courses, certifications, and real-world exercises.
