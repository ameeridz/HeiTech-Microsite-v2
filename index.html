AwanHeiTech

Malaysia Premier Cloud Service Provider

A product from HeiTech Padu Berhad (HeiTech), AwanHeiTech brings to the market a reliable, robust and revolutionary suite of premium cloud solutions to meet your every business need.

Having delivered and maintained mission critical systems to clients across the globe, our enterprise level network and data center infrastructure cater to enterprise scale organisations, enabling our cloud solutions to meet the challenges you face daily.

With the experience of managing a Data Centre and Disaster Recovery site and also being a successful managed services provider, it is only natural for HeiTech to move into Cloud Services offering.

Leveraging on its in-house Managed Services, HeiTech launched its Cloud Services under the brandname of AwanHeiTech in 2011, combining cloud facilities and managed services offerings such as IaaS, BaaS, DRaaS, PaaS, SecaaS & SaaS.
